Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

Onmind is built by Onmind Studios on a simple principle: your data should stay yours. This policy explains, plainly, what Onmind does and doesn't do with your information.

The short version

  • Onmind runs on your device. We do not operate a server that receives your conversations.
  • There is no account and no sign-up.
  • We collect no analytics, use no tracking, and do not sell or share your data.
  • Onmind does not use any third-party AI service — no OpenAI, Google, or Anthropic.
  • Onmind makes no network requests for its features — it works fully offline.

How Onmind works

Onmind uses Apple's Foundation Models framework — the on-device large language model included with Apple Intelligence. When you send a message, the response is generated locally on your device's Neural Engine. Your prompts and conversations are not transmitted to us or any third party for generation. Onmind functions in airplane mode.

Information stored on your device

The following stay on your device, protected by iOS data protection, and are never uploaded by us:

  • Your conversation history and any facts Onmind remembers about you.
  • Generated images, imported documents, and photos you share with the app.
  • Your settings and preferences.

You can delete individual chats, clear all history, and clear saved memory at any time in the app, and you may add an optional Face ID lock. Removing the app removes its on-device data.

Device permissions

Onmind asks for certain Apple system permissions only when you use the related feature, and the data is used solely on your device:

  • Microphone & Speech Recognition — for hands-free voice mode.
  • Reminders & Calendar — to create reminders and events when you ask.
  • Contacts — to look up a contact when you ask.
  • Photos — to read or describe an image you choose to share.

You can review or revoke these at any time in the iOS Settings app.

No network requests

Onmind does not contact any server to function. There is no web search, no analytics endpoint, and no third-party AI service. Everything — chat, images, voice, documents — happens on your device.

Purchases

Onmind is a one-time, paid app purchased through the App Store. Payment is processed entirely by Apple; we never see or receive your payment details.

Children's privacy

Onmind does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. We do not knowingly gather data from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the date above and post the revised version here.
