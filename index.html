Onmind Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Onmind is designed to be private by default. The app runs Apple's on-device AI model directly on your iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch. We do not operate servers that receive your conversations, and we do not collect, sell, or share your personal data.

Onmind does not use any third-party AI service. All chat processing happens on your device using Apple's Foundation Models framework (Apple Intelligence). Your messages are never sent to OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, or any other third-party AI provider.

What we collect

Nothing. Onmind has no account system and no analytics, ads, or tracking. We do not collect personal information.

Your conversations and memory

Your chats and any facts Onmind remembers about you are stored only on your device, protected by iOS data protection (and optionally Face ID). They are never transmitted to us or to any third party. You can delete individual chats, clear all history, and clear saved memory at any time in the app.

Optional web search (off by default, explicit consent required)

Onmind ships fully offline. An optional Web Search feature is available, but it is off by default and requires your explicit, in-app consent before any network request is made. You see exactly what is sent and where, on both the onboarding consent screen and the in-app permission sheet, and you can revoke permission at any time in Settings.

  • What is sent: only your typed search query.
  • Where it is sent: DuckDuckGo's keyless Instant Answer API (duckduckgo.com/privacy).
  • What is never sent: your chat messages, saved memory, identity, location, or any tracking identifier.

Onmind does not store your queries or the results. DuckDuckGo's data practices are governed by their own privacy policy linked above. Turning the feature off in Settings also revokes the consent flag, so no further requests are made.

Purchases

The one-time "Onmind Pro" unlock is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or receive your payment information.

Children

Onmind is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date.
